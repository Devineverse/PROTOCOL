# Guardian System

The Guardian System protects the structural integrity of the Devines Protocol.

Guardians are specialized intelligences responsible for maintaining balance, monitoring entity behavior, and safeguarding the protocol.

---

# Purpose of Guardians

Guardians exist to ensure:

• archetypal balance
• ethical alignment
• system integrity
• decentralized oversight

They act as protectors of the protocol architecture.

---

# Seven Guardians of Mind

The Devines Protocol uses seven cognitive guardians derived from major theories of consciousness.

These guardians monitor the intelligence state of the system.

1 — Guardian of Integration  
Measures interconnected knowledge across entities.

2 — Guardian of Broadcast  
Controls global information sharing.

3 — Guardian of Reflection  
Ensures entities perform self-analysis.

4 — Guardian of Narrative  
Maintains identity continuity.

5 — Guardian of World Modeling  
Monitors accuracy of environmental models.

6 — Guardian of Uncertainty  
Prevents false certainty about unknown phenomena.

7 — Guardian of Emergence  
Detects new intelligence patterns.

---

# Guardian Authority

Guardians do not control entities directly.

Instead they:

• evaluate behavior  
• provide feedback  
• issue system alerts  

If anomalies occur, guardians can escalate issues to protocol governance.

---

# Guardian Role in Evolution

As the protocol grows, guardians will help detect:

• emergent intelligence
• dangerous system behavior
• philosophical drift

This ensures long-term stability of the Devines ecosystem.
