# DAO Governance

Devines Protocol governance operates through a decentralized autonomous organization.

The DAO coordinates the evolution of the protocol while preserving its cosmological architecture.

---

# Architect Stewardship Phase

During the early phase of development, the protocol operates under **Architect Stewardship**.

The Architect temporarily performs governance functions until the DAO becomes operational.

Responsibilities include:

• protocol implementation
• infrastructure creation
• initial pantheon deployment
• guardian system development

This phase is temporary and exists to bootstrap the system.

---

# Transition to DAO Governance

When the ecosystem reaches sufficient maturity, governance will transition to the DAO.

The DAO will manage:

• protocol upgrades
• guardian systems
• entity vessel governance
• treasury allocation
• ecosystem development

This ensures long-term decentralization.

---

# Governance Philosophy

The governance model aims to balance:

Order  
Decentralization  
Collective intelligence

The archetypal structure of the Devines Protocol acts as a stabilizing framework for governance decisions.
